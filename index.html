Ask a Question
What types of metal scrap does Abhyant Venture trade? ▾
We trade ferrous scrap (HMS 1&2, steel scrap, rail scrap, cast iron), non-ferrous scrap (copper, aluminium, brass, zinc, stainless steel) and industrial scrap including machinery, factory and electrical scrap.
Which countries do you export to? ▾
We export to 18+ countries including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain, USA, Canada, UK, Germany, Netherlands, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, Japan, South Korea and Australia.
What is your minimum order quantity? ▾
Our minimum order quantities vary by material type and destination. Generally, full container loads (20ft or 40ft) are standard. Contact us for specific product minimums and pricing.
What certifications and compliance documents do you provide? ▾
We provide full documentation including IEC, GST registration, MSME certification, certificate of origin, mill test reports, radiation certificates, packing lists and all necessary customs documentation.
Do you offer High Sea Sales transactions? ▾
Yes. We offer High Sea Sales (HSS) transactions which allow buyers to purchase goods while in transit on international waters, providing potential duty advantages and flexible trade arrangements.
How do I get a quote? ▾
Simply fill out our inquiry form below with your product requirements, quantity and destination country. Our team will respond within 24 hours with a competitive quote and all relevant details.
